Bomberos

The Bomberos are a voluntary fire, ambulance and rescue service who raise much of their own funding. They go to the aid of tourists as well as Guatemalans and there is no charge for their help.  It is a wonderful organisation who we all are proud to help.  Pass It On makes donations, supports the services fund raisers and gave places on our Water Safety Workshops.
